# Code of Conduct

Rute Bayar is a community project. We want collaboration here to be respectful, useful, and safe for contributors at all experience levels.

## Our Standards

Examples of encouraged behavior:

- Be kind, patient, and constructive.
- Assume good intent while still being clear about risks or bugs.
- Give feedback about the work, not the person.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and its users.
- Credit ideas and contributions from others.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information or credentials.
- Repeated off-topic disruption.
- Personal attacks in issues, reviews, or discussions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that violate this code of conduct. Repeated or severe violations may lead to temporary or permanent bans from project spaces.

## Reporting

If you see behavior that makes the project unsafe or unwelcoming, contact the maintainers through the repository owner channel:

https://github.com/pendig/rute-bayar

Reports should include context, links, and screenshots when relevant. Please avoid exposing private information publicly.
