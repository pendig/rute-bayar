Rute Bayar keeps provider expansion modular. New providers should add adapters, status mapping, webhook verification, forwarding compatibility, docs, and E2E notes without changing the core payment model more than necessary.

DOKU is already available in the release update. The next provider families tracked in the roadmap are Flip Business and Duitku.
